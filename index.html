About

Hi there, my name is Janaka. I'm a full-stack web developer in Melbourne, Australia. I am passionate about building beautiful, interactive web apps giving the best user experience. I spend most of the day in front of a computer unless I hang out with friends for a picnic or party. Besides the web, I'm interested in new technologies and DIYing, especially 3D printing and CNC-related stuff.

I was born and lived in Sri Lanka until I moved to Melbourne in 2022. I completed my bachelor's in Chemical & Process Engineering in 2017. After graduation, I worked as a process engineer for four years in the Sugar manufacturing industry, which is more of an executive role focused on managing the workforce and making decisions.

In the fall of 2021, I started learning to code with Java programming language as a self-learner. Since then, I fell in love with coding and realized I should start a new career as a developer. After moving to Melbourne, I joined Le Wagon coding bootcamp to broaden my knowledge.

I primarily use Ruby on Rails for building full-stack web applications and React/Redux or vanilla JavaScript for front-end development. If you plan to build a web application, collaborate on something cool, or want to say hi, shoot me a message. Let's work together!
